Matter is any substance that has [[mass]] and takes up space by having volume

Based on the definition above, it means that all that exist is made up of matter (including thoughts. Because where would they be otherwise?).

Atoms and substances are made up of matter, which is pretty much everything that exist.

The amount of matter in an object is measured by its mass

### Intensive
Property that is present regardless of the amount of matter present
Samples:
- Pressure
- Temperature

### Extensive
Properties depending on the amount of matter present in a sample
Samples:
- Weight
- Volume
- Height
