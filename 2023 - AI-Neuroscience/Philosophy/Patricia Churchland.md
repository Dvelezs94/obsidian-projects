# Patricia Churchland
![[Pasted image 20220922131804.png]]
Canadian-American [Analityc Philosoper](obsidian://open?vault=Neuroscience&file=Philosophy%2FAnayltic%20philosophy) noted for the contributions to the [[neurophilosophy]] and the [[Philosophy of mind]].

> To understand the mind you have to understand the brain
> Patricia Churchland

She applies findings from neuroscience to address traditional philosophical questions about [[Thought]], [[knowledge]], [[free will]], [[Consciousness]] and ethics.\
