# Philosophy of Mind
Philosophy of mind is a branch of subfield of [[Metaphysics]] concerned the nature of the [[Mind]].

It is the field concerned with the part of us that thinks, feels, perceives and wills, imagines and dreams.

It wants to know how does the mind work, what its powers are and how it relates to the physical body and to the rest of the world.

## Three basic questions in the philosophy of mind
1. What is the relationship between mind and bodies
2. What makes a mind *that* particular mind (personal identity)?
3. Does consciousness exist before/after death?
