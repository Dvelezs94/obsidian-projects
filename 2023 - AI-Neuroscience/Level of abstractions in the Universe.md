Here is a list of levels of abstractions that we know in the universe. Each abstraction is just a group from the previous abstraction
1. Elementary - quarks
2. Atomic level (group of quarks) - Atoms, electrons
3. Chemical Level (group of atoms)- H (hidrogen), O (oxygen) 
4. Molecular level (group of chemicals) - H20 (Water)
5. Celular level (group of molecules) - Prokaryotic / Eukaryotic Cells
6. Multi-celular level (group of cells) - Animals, Plants
7. Ecosystem (Group of multi-celular beings)
8. Planets (Group of Ecosystems)
10. Galaxies (Group of planets)
