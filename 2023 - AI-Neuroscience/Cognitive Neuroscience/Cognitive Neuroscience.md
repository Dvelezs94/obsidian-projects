# Cognitive Neuroscience
Cognitive Neuroscience is a subfield of [[Neuroscience]] that studies the biological processes that underline human [[Cognition]]. This field *studies the neural connections within the human brain* to determine how the brain achieves the functions it performs.

This field is considered cross-disciplinary because it combines biological sciences (biology / neuroscience )with behavioral sciences (psychology, psyquiatry).
