## List of living organisms
Living organisms are divided into 7 different "kingdoms" (categories)

1. Bacteria
2. Archaea (single-cell prokaryotes)
3. Protozoa (single-cell eukaryotes)
5. Chromista (multi-cellular eukaryotes)
6. Plants (photosynthetic eukaryotes)
7. Fungi
8. Animalia
