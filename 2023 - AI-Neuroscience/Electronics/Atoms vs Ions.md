An ion is an atom or molecule thay has gained or lost one or more [[Valence Electrons]]

Examples of ions
Hydroxide $OH^-$


Ions could be form a single atom particle or from more than one. 

**Monoatomic** ions are formed from a single atom. Sample is Hidrogen ion $H^+$
**Polyatomic** ions need 2 or more atoms, chich is the same as a molecule. Sample is hydronium $H_3O^+$
